Coastal Specialists

Built for Peninsula Conditions

Salt spray is the enemy of clean windows on the Mornington Peninsula. It doesn't just make glass look cloudy — over time, mineral deposits etch into the surface and become increasingly difficult to remove. Living near the bay or ocean means your windows need more frequent attention than inland properties.

We understand the specific challenges of cleaning in coastal environments and use the right products and methods to combat salt residue, mineral build-up, and the grime that comes with sea breezes.

Recommended Frequency

Residential — 3 to 6 Months

Standard residential homes benefit from a clean every 3–6 months to prevent salt and grime build-up.

Commercial Properties

Commercial — 1 to 3 Months

High foot-traffic commercial premises, shopfronts, and offices typically require more frequent cleaning.

Beachfront & Waterfront

Beachfront — More Often

Properties directly facing the bay or ocean accumulate salt spray far faster and may need monthly attention.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you clean inside and outside? +
Yes. We offer interior and exterior window cleaning as standard. You can choose to have both done or just the exterior if preferred. Interior cleaning does require someone to be home to provide access.
Can you clean windows on a double-storey home? +
Yes. We carry ladders and appropriate equipment to safely reach upper-level windows on double-storey homes. For multi-storey commercial buildings, we use water-fed pole systems or other access methods depending on height and building type.
How often should I have my windows cleaned? +
For most homes on the Peninsula, every 3–6 months keeps windows looking clean and prevents salt and mineral deposits from etching into the glass. Beachfront properties, commercial shopfronts, and properties surrounded by trees may need more frequent cleaning.
Do you clean flyscreens as well? +
Yes, flyscreen cleaning is available on request. We remove, clean, and refit screens as part of a full window clean. Dirty screens can transfer grime back onto clean glass, so including them gives you a better end result.
